## KEY HIRING TRENDS RELEVANT TO PACVOLT

### 1. Massive Manufacturing Expansion
Multiple competitors are investing heavily in US manufacturing:
- **Eaton**: $100M expansion, 218 new jobs in voltage regulator manufacturing
- **ABB**: $110M US investment, ~200 new jobs in grid components
- **ERMCO**: 500+ new jobs in Arizona transformer plant
- **Hitachi Energy**: $4.1B US transformer manufacturing investment
- **Reinhausen**: $3.3M expansion for 25% capacity increase

**Implication for PacVolt**: The voltage regulation and transformer market is growing rapidly. Competitors are scaling up — PacVolt should consider its own manufacturing capacity plans.

### 2. Firmware & Controls Engineering Demand
Many companies are hiring firmware engineers, controls engineers, and software developers for grid equipment — confirming the trend toward smart, connected voltage regulation.

### 3. Field Service Growth
Strong demand for field service engineers across competitors suggests growing installed bases and the importance of post-sale support.

### 4. Sales Coverage Expansion
Reinhausen and others are hiring remote sales managers across the US — expanding their cooperative market coverage. PacVolt should monitor where competitors are placing sales resources.

### 5. Renewable Integration Roles
FlexGen, RWE, Silicon Ranch, and others are hiring heavily for renewable energy integration — the same market dynamic that drives demand for PacVolt's LVR products.
